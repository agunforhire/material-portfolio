About Me

A lifelong tinkerer and technology enthusiast based in the San Francisco Bay Area. After 12 years in the IT industry, he's trying to expand beyond switches and server rooms and into the coding realm.

He is currently in a Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp at UC Berkeley, but has a penchant for Javascript, Jquery, React and Node. In what remains of his free time, he enjoys live music, cycling, good food and good company.
